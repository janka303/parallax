ABOUT

Parallax embodies the rare moment when you and another person have a parallel experience or thought, making you aware of collective consciousness. It allows users to explore a deeper connection between themselves and the universe. Taking two people and allowing them to focus on a deeper connection allows them to explore the deeper nature of that feeling when you think about other people in the world doing or thinking about the same topics as you are at the same moment in time.
